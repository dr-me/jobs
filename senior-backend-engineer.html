Senior Backend Engineer

Dr Me is looking for experienced backend engineers to work on its products.

Company

Dr Me is a telemedicine startup based in Melbourne, Australia. We make it possible for patients to see a doctor from the convenience of their smart phone.

Our tech team is small but diverse. We eat lunch together most days and try to keep a positive, encouraging environment. We are often teaching each other and staying up to date on the Latest New Thing™.

We care about tooling and providing the best development experience we can. We move quickly but do regular iteration planning with the whole team so everyone stays on the same page. We’re not afraid to ask questions when we’re stuck and respect that uninterrupted work can be the best kind of work.

Role

The core of our backend application is Ruby on Rails, which is deployed across multiple AZ’s in AWS. Where we can we are refactoring our application into self-contained components and we’re always looking to improve our tooling and process.

We are looking for a senior backend engineer who has worked with Ruby on Rails in a production environment.

You should be fluent in:

  • Ruby/Rails
  • RSpec

You should be familiar with:

  • Linux
  • PostgreSQL
  • Docker
  • Build pipelines

The ideal candidate would also:

  • Have experience with DC/OS, Marathon, security
  • Enjoy keeping tabs on new technology and platforms

Benefits

  • Flexible working environment – work remote 2 days a week
  • Join a diverse team!
  • 7 minute walk from Richmond station and Swan St
  • Salary range is $100–130k with options but negotiable for the right person
