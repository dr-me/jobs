Product Manager

Dr Me is looking for a product manager to grow and foster its products.

Company

Dr Me is a telemedicine startup based in Melbourne, Australia. We make it possible for patients to see a doctor from the convenience of their smart phone.

Our tech team is small but diverse. We eat lunch together most days and try to keep a positive, encouraging environment. We are often teaching each other and staying up to date on the Latest New Thing™.

We care about tooling and providing the best development experience we can. We move quickly but do regular iteration planning with the whole team so everyone stays on the same page. We’re not afraid to ask questions when we’re stuck and respect that uninterrupted work can be the best kind of work.

Role

We are looking for someone to manage, develop and guide our products. You’ll be expected to know how to write a product specification, conduct market research and be able to generate technical and non-technical requirements.

As the go-between for the tech, marketing and operations team you’ll be helping us determine price points, scope out timelines and time-integrated plans for product and help us to meet key deliverables.

Ideally you will have worked in startup or tech product environment with experience in balancing technical limitations, business opportunities and achievable deadlines.

You should be familiar with:

  • Trello
  • Gantt Charts
  • Spreadsheets

Benefits

  • Flexible working environment – work remote 2 days a week
  • Join a diverse team!
  • 7 minute walk from Richmond station and Swan St
  • Salary range is $120–160 with options depending on experience
