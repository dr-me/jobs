Product Designer

Dr Me is looking for an experienced product designer to work on its products.

Company

Dr Me is a telemedicine startup based in Melbourne, Australia. We make it possible for patients to see a doctor from the convenience of their smart phone.

Our product engineering team is small but diverse. We eat lunch together most days and try to keep a positive, encouraging environment. We are often teaching each other and staying up to date on the Latest New Thing™.

We care about product and strive for continuously improving our product. We move quickly but do regular iteration planning with the whole team so everyone stays on the same page. We’re not afraid to ask questions when we’re stuck and respect that uninterrupted work can be the best kind of work.

Role

Your focus will be designing how patients and doctors interact with our platform; all the way from the high-level feature design to the details in the UI.

You'll be doing:

  • Designing new features and improving existing ones
  • Usability research
  • Designing and testing user flows
  • Building interactive prototypes
  • Understanding and clarifying business requirements with relevant stakeholders
  • Working closely with engineers to realise features

You should have:

  • 2+ years experience designing user-facing experiences on mobile and web
  • Strong understanding and ability to apply design principles
  • Solid knowledge of UI/UX prototyping tools

The ideal candidate would also:

  • Have HTML/CSS/JS experience
  • Have graphical design experience

Benefits

  • Flexible working environment – work remote 2 days a week
  • Join a diverse team!
  • 7 minute walk from Richmond station and Swan St
  • Salary range is $90–110k with options but negotiable for the right person
